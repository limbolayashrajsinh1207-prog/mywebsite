About FarmBid

FarmBid was founded with a simple mission: to connect local farmers directly with buyers, creating a transparent and efficient marketplace for agricultural products. We believe in supporting sustainable farming and providing a fair platform for both producers and consumers. Our auction model ensures that farmers get a competitive price for their hard work, and buyers get access to the freshest, highest-quality crops available.

We are committed to building a community based on trust, quality, and mutual respect. From small-scale organic farms to large-scale producers, our platform is designed to serve the diverse needs of the agricultural industry.

Bidding Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a fair and transparent experience for all users, please review the following rules before participating in any auction.

  1. Registration: All bidders must have a registered account and be logged in to place a bid.
  2. Bidding Increments: Bids must be placed in whole dollar increments. The minimum bid is $5.00 above the current bid.
  3. Final Bid: The highest bid at the end of the auction period is considered the winning bid. All sales are final.
  4. Payment: Payment must be made within 48 hours of the auction's end. Failure to pay may result in a ban from future auctions.
  5. Disputes: Any disputes regarding an auction must be submitted to our support team within 24 hours of the auction's close.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid?

First, you need to register and log in to your account. Navigate to the auction page of the crop you are interested in and enter your bid amount in the provided field.

What happens if I win an auction?

If you win, you will receive an email notification. You will then have 48 hours to complete the payment. After payment is confirmed, the seller will arrange for delivery or pickup of the crops.

Is there a fee to use the site?

Registration and browsing are free. A small service fee is applied to the winning bid to cover the costs of running the platform.
